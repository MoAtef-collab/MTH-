البروتوكولات الطبية 2026

Pneumonia

Pneumonia Protocols

تحميل PDF
Nosocomial Pneumonia (HAP)

NO risk factors for MDR:

1ST line Ceftazidime 1-2g/8hr OR Cefepime 2g/8-12hr OR Pip/Tazo 4.5g/6hr

2nd line Meropenem 1g/8hr

ADD anti-MRSA if risks present:
1st: Vancomycin (15-20mg/kg/8-12hr) | 2nd: Linezolid (600mg/12hr) | 3rd: Teicoplanin

WITH risk factors for MDR:

(Ventilatory support, Septic shock, IV Abx past 90 days)

1ST line Meropenem 1g/8hr | 2nd line Imipenem-cilastatin 500mg/6hrs

+ PLUS:

Antipseudomonal fluoroquinolone (Levo / Cipro 400mg/8hr) OR Aminoglycosides (Amikacin)

+ PLUS Anti-MRSA:

Vancomycin (1st) OR Linezolid (2nd)

Duration: 7-14 days

Nosocomial Pneumonia (VAP)

NO risk factors for MDR:

Same as HAP No-risk protocol (Ceftazidime, Cefepime, or Pip/Tazo)

WITH risk factors for MDR:

(ARDS, Septic shock, IV Abx past 90 days, ≥5 days hosp, Acute RRT)

1ST line Meropenem 1g/8hr | 2nd line Imipenem 500mg/6hrs

+ PLUS:

Fluoroquinolone (Levo/Cipro) OR Aminoglycosides (Amikacin)

+ PLUS Anti-MRSA:

Vancomycin (1st) OR Linezolid (2nd)

Duration: 7-14 days

Community Acquired (CAP)

Non-severe & No MRSA/Ps risk:

1st Line Ampicillin-sulbactam 3g/6hr + Clarithromycin 500mg/12hr

OR Cefotaxime 1-2g/8hr / Ceftriaxone 1g/24hr + Clarithromycin

2nd Line Moxifloxacin 400mg once OR Levofloxacin 750mg once

Severe with No MRSA/Ps risk:

1st Line (Ampicillin-sulbactam OR Cefotaxime OR Ceftriaxone) PLUS (Clarithromycin OR Moxifloxacin OR Levofloxacin)

Risk of Pseudomonas & MRSA:

1st Line Ceftazidime 1-2g/8hr OR Cefepime 1-2g/8-12hr

2nd Line Pip/Tazo 4.5g/6hr OR Meropenem 1g/8hr

+ PLUS (Pseudomonas coverage):

Levofloxacin 750mg OR Amikacin 15mg/kg (+ Azithromycin 500mg if Amikacin used)

+ PLUS (MRSA coverage):

Vancomycin (15-20mg/kg/8-12hr) OR Linezolid OR Teicoplanin

Duration: 5-7 days
