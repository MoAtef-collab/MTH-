Pneumonia

Pneumonia Protocols

تحميل PDF
Nosocomial Pneumonia (HAP)

If NO risk factors for MDR micro-organisms:

1st Line Ceftazidime: 1-2 gm/8hr OR Cefepime: 2gm/8-12 hr OR Piperacillin/Tazobactam: 4.5gm/6hr
2nd Line Meropenem: 1 gm/8hr

ADD anti-MRSA if any risk factors are present:

  • 1st Line Vancomycin: 15 to 20 mg/kg/dose every 8 to 12 hours
  • 2nd Line Linezolid: 600mg/12hr
  • 3rd Line Teicoplanin: 6mg/Kg/12hr for 3 doses followed by 6 mg/kg once daily.

If risk factors for MDR micro-organisms present:

  • Need for ventilatory support
  • Septic shock
  • Receipt of IV antibiotics within past 90 days
1st Line Meropenem: 1gm/8h
2nd Line Imipenem-cilastatin: 500mg/6hrs
+ PLUS Antipseudomonal: Fluoroquinolone (Levofloxacin/Ciprofloxacin 400mg/8hr) OR Aminoglycosides (Amikacin)
+ PLUS Anti-MRSA:
1st Line Vancomycin: 15-20 mg/kg/dose q8-12h
2nd Line Linezolid: 600mg/12hr

Duration: 7 days (may extend to 10-14 days).

Nosocomial Pneumonia (VAP)

If NO risk factors for MDR:

1st Line Ceftazidime 1-2 gm/8hr OR Cefepime 2gm/8-12hr OR Pip/Tazo 4.5gm/6hr

2nd Line Meropenem 1gm/8hr

ADD Anti-MRSA if risk factors present:

1st Vancomycin 15-20 mg/kg/dose q8-12h

2nd Linezolid 600mg/12hr

3rd Teicoplanin 6mg/kg/12hr (3 doses) then 6mg/kg daily.

If risk factors for MDR present:

  • ARDS preceding VAP
  • Septic shock
  • IV antibiotics within past 90 days
  • ≥5 days hospitalization prior to VAP
  • Acute renal replacement therapy prior to VAP

1st Line Meropenem 1gm/8h

2nd Line Imipenem-cilastatin 500mg/6hrs

+ PLUS Antipseudomonal: Fluoroquinolone (Levofloxacin, Ciprofloxacin) OR Aminoglycosides (Amikacin)
+ PLUS Anti-MRSA: Vancomycin (15-20 mg/kg q8-12h) OR Linezolid (600mg/12hr)

Duration: 7 days (extend to 10-14 in severe cases).

Community Acquired (CAP) - Ward/ICU

Non-severe (No MRSA/Pseudomonas risk):

1st Line
  • • Ampicillin-sulbactam 3gm/6hr + Clarithromycin 500mg/12hr
  • • OR Cefotaxime 1-2gm/8hr (or Ceftriaxone 1gm/24hr) + Clarithromycin 500mg/12hr
2nd Line (Monotherapy)

Moxifloxacin 400mg once OR Levofloxacin 750mg once daily.

Severe (No MRSA/Pseudomonas risk):

1st Line

Ampicillin-sulbactam 3gm/6hr OR Cefotaxime/Ceftriaxone

--- PLUS ---

Clarithromycin 500mg/12hr OR Moxifloxacin 400mg OR Levofloxacin 750mg

Risk of Pseudomonas & MRSA:

1st Line Ceftazidime 1-2 gm/8hr OR Cefepime 1-2gm/8-12hr

2nd Line Piperacillin/Tazobactam 4.5gm/6hr OR Meropenem 1gm/8hr

+ PLUS (Choose One):
  • Levofloxacin 750mg once
  • Amikacin 15mg/kg divided / 8-12hr (If Amikacin used, add Azithromycin 500mg/24hr)
+ PLUS Anti-MRSA (Choose One):
  • Vancomycin 15-20 mg/kg q8-12h
  • Linezolid 600mg/12hr
  • Teicoplanin 6mg/kg/12hr (3 doses) then 6mg/kg daily

Duration: 5-7 days
