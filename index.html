البروتوكولات الطبية 2026

Nosocomial Pneumonia (HAP)

No risk factors for MDR micro-organisms:

1st Line Ceftazidime 1-2g/8h OR Cefepime 2g/8-12h OR Piperacillin/Tazobactam 4.5g/6h

2nd Line Meropenem 1g/8h

ADD anti-MRSA if risk factors present:
- 1st: Vancomycin 15-20mg/kg/dose every 8-12h
- 2nd: Linezolid 600mg/12h
- 3rd: Teicoplanin 6mg/kg/12h (3 doses) then 6mg/kg daily

With risk factors for MDR (Ventilation, Shock, IV Abx < 90 days):

1st Line Meropenem 1g/8h

2nd Line Imipenem-cilastatin 500mg/6h

PLUS:

- Antipseudomonal Fluoroquinolone (Levofloxacin/Ciprofloxacin 400mg/8h) OR Aminoglycosides (Amikacin)

PLUS Anti-MRSA:

- Vancomycin (1st) or Linezolid (2nd)

Duration: 7 days (may extend to 10-14 days)

Nosocomial Pneumonia (VAP)

No risk factors for MDR:

Same as HAP protocol (Ceftazidime, Cefepime, or Pip/Tazo).

With risk factors for MDR (ARDS, Shock, Abx < 90d, Hosp > 5d, RRT):

1st Line Meropenem 1g/8h PLUS Antipseudomonal (Quinolone/Amikacin) PLUS Anti-MRSA (Vanco/Linezolid)

Duration: 7 days (up to 10-14 in severe cases)

Community Acquired Pneumonia (CAP)

Non-severe (No MRSA/Ps risk):

1st: Ampicillin-sulbactam 3g/6h + Clarithromycin 500mg/12h OR Ceftriaxone 1g/24h + Clarithromycin.

2nd: Moxifloxacin 400mg OR Levofloxacin 750mg (Monotherapy).

Severe (No MRSA/Ps risk):

Ampicillin-sulbactam OR Ceftriaxone PLUS Clarithromycin OR Quinolone.

Risk of Pseudomonas & MRSA:

1st: Ceftazidime or Cefepime | 2nd: Pip/Tazo or Meropenem

PLUS:

Levofloxacin 750mg OR Amikacin 15mg/kg (if Amikacin, add Azithromycin)

PLUS Anti-MRSA:

Vancomycin OR Linezolid OR Teicoplanin.

Duration: 5-7 days
