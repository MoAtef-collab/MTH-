Pneumonia

Pneumonia Protocols

تحميل PDF
Nosocomial Pneumonia (HAP)

NO risk factors for MDR:

1ST line Ceftazidime 1-2g/8hr OR Cefepime 2g/8-12hr OR Pip/Tazo 4.5g/6hr

2nd line Meropenem 1g/8hr

ADD anti-MRSA if risks: Vancomycin (1st) | Linezolid (2nd) | Teicoplanin (3rd)

WITH risk factors for MDR:

1ST line Meropenem 1g/8h | 2nd line Imipenem 500mg/6hrs

+ PLUS Quinolone (Levo/Cipro) OR Amikacin

+ PLUS Anti-MRSA (Vancomycin or Linezolid)

Nosocomial Pneumonia (VAP)

NO MDR risk factors:

Same as HAP No-risk line (Ceftazidime, Cefepime, or Pip/Tazo)

WITH MDR risk factors:

(ARDS, Septic shock, IV Abx past 90d, etc.)

1ST line Meropenem | 2nd line Imipenem

+ PLUS Antipseudomonal OR Amikacin

+ PLUS Anti-MRSA (Vancomycin or Linezolid)

Community Acquired (CAP)

Non-severe:

1st Ampicillin-sulbactam + Clarithromycin OR Ceftriaxone + Clarithromycin

Severe:

(Ampicillin-sulbactam OR Ceftriaxone) PLUS (Clarithromycin OR Moxi/Levo)

Pseudomonas & MRSA Risk:

1st Ceftazidime or Cefepime | 2nd Pip/Tazo or Meropenem

+ PLUS (Levo OR Amikacin+Azithro) PLUS (Vancomycin OR Linezolid OR Teicoplanin)
