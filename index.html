Pneumonia

Pneumonia Protocols

PDF
Nosocomial Pneumonia (HAP)

If NO risk factors for MDR:

1ST line Ceftazidime 1-2g/8hr OR Cefepime 2g/8-12hr OR Pip/Tazo 4.5g/6hr

2nd line Meropenem 1g/8hr

ADD anti-MRSA if risk factors present (Vancomycin, Linezolid, or Teicoplanin)

If risk factors for MDR:

1ST line Meropenem 1g/8h | 2nd line Imipenem 500mg/6hrs

+ PLUS: Levofloxacin / Ciprofloxacin 400mg/8hr OR Amikacin

+ PLUS: Anti-MRSA (Vancomycin OR Linezolid)

Nosocomial Pneumonia (VAP)

Risk factors for MDR (VAP):

1ST line Meropenem 1g/8h | 2nd line Imipenem 500mg/6hrs

+ PLUS: Levofloxacin / Ciprofloxacin OR Amikacin

+ PLUS: Anti-MRSA (Vancomycin OR Linezolid)

Community Acquired (CAP)

Non-severe:

Ampicillin-sulbactam 3g/6hr + Clarithromycin 500mg/12hr

Pseudomonas & MRSA Risk:

Ceftazidime / Cefepime PLUS Levo / Amikacin PLUS Vancomycin / Linezolid
